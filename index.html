Welcome to Faith Community Center

A place where faith grows, hope flourishes, and love transforms lives. Join us in building a stronger community together.

About Us

Faith Community Center is dedicated to nurturing spiritual growth, fostering meaningful connections, and serving our community with compassion and purpose.

Worship Together

Experience uplifting worship services that inspire and strengthen your faith. Our community gathers to celebrate, pray, and grow closer to God and one another.

Fellowship & Growth

Build lasting relationships through small groups, Bible studies, and community events. We believe in walking together on our spiritual journey.

Serve with Love

Make a difference in our community through outreach programs, volunteer opportunities, and acts of compassion that reflect God's love.

Scholarship Program

Empowering Future Leaders

Our scholarship program supports promising students in our community who demonstrate academic excellence, leadership potential, and a commitment to service.

Award Amount: Up to $5,000 per academic year

Eligibility: High school seniors and current college students who are active members of our faith community and maintain a minimum 3.0 GPA.

Application Deadline: March 31, 2026
